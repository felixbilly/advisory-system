Edit Q&A

What do I need to do to declare a major?
To declare a major, you need to meet with an advisor in the department of the major you want to declare. They will go over the requirements and help you fill out the necessary paperwork.
What classes do I need to take to graduate?
The specific classes you need to take to graduate depend on your major and any minor or concentration you may have. You can find a list of the required courses in the course catalog or by meeting with your academic advisor.
What should I do if I'm struggling in a class?
If you're struggling in a class, the first step is to talk to your professor or TA. They may be able to offer you extra help or advice on how to improve. You can also seek out tutoring or academic support services on campus.
How do I know which classes fulfill my general education requirements?
You can find a list of the general education requirements and the classes that fulfill them in the course catalog. Your academic advisor can also help you determine which classes you still need to take to fulfill your requirements.
